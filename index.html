Melanotan 2 (MT2)

GHK-Cu is a naturally occurring copper complex of the tripeptide glycyl-L-histidyl-L-lysine. The peptide has a strong affinity for copper(II) and was first isolated from human plasma. It is available in 50mg vials for topical formulation research.
